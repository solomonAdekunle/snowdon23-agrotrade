Our Journey

2018

Foundation

Snowdon23 AgroTrade was established with a vision to transform Nigerian agriculture exports and connect local farmers to global markets.

2019

First International Partnership

Successfully established partnerships with distributors in UK and Europe, exporting our first 50 tons of premium cashew kernels.

2023

Obtained international quality certifications and began exporting cocoa beans and coffee beans, serving 100+ satisfied clients.

2025

Industry Leader

Now exporting 500+ tons annually to 15+ countries, recognized as one of Nigeria's most reliable agricultural export companies.
