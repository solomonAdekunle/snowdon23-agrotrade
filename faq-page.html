What is the minimum order quantity? +

Our minimum order quantity varies by product but typically starts at 1 metric ton for most items. For cashew kernels and sesame seeds, the MOQ is 1MT. For bulk buyers, we offer flexible quantities and better pricing. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How do I place an order? +

Placing an order is simple: (1) Contact us via email, WhatsApp, or our contact form with your product requirements, (2) We'll provide a detailed quote including pricing, specifications, and delivery timeline, (3) Once you approve, we'll send a proforma invoice, (4) Upon payment confirmation, we process and ship your order.

Can I request product samples? +

Yes! We offer samples for serious buyers. Sample costs and courier fees are borne by the buyer, but we deduct these costs from your first bulk order. Samples are typically 500g-1kg per product and help you verify quality before committing to larger quantities.

How do you ensure product quality? +

Quality is our top priority. We implement strict quality control at every stage: (1) Sourcing only from certified farms, (2) Manual sorting and inspection, (3) Laboratory testing for moisture content, impurities, and contamination, (4) Proper storage in climate-controlled facilities, (5) Final inspection before packaging. All products meet international export standards.

Do you have quality certifications? +

Yes, our products are HACCP compliant and meet international export standards. We can provide certificates of analysis, phytosanitary certificates, and certificates of origin with each shipment. For organic certification or other specific requirements, please contact us in advance.

What if I receive damaged or substandard products? +

Customer satisfaction is paramount. If you receive damaged or substandard products, contact us immediately with photos and details. We'll investigate and resolve the issue through replacement, refund, or credit toward your next order, depending on the circumstances. All shipments are insured for your protection.

Which countries do you ship to? +

We currently export to 15+ countries across Europe, Asia, North America, and the Middle East. Our primary markets include the UK, Germany, Netherlands, UAE, Canada, USA, China, and India. If your country isn't listed, contact us—we're always expanding our reach.

How long does shipping take? +

Delivery times vary by destination and shipping method. Typically: Sea freight to Europe takes 4-6 weeks, to Asia 3-5 weeks, to North America 5-7 weeks. Air freight is available for urgent orders (7-14 days). Lead time includes order processing (3-5 days), customs clearance, and final delivery.

What are the shipping terms? +

We offer flexible shipping terms: FOB (Free On Board), CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight), and CFR (Cost and Freight). We can also arrange door-to-door delivery for established clients. Discuss your preferred terms during the quotation process.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept: Bank Wire Transfer (T/T), Letter of Credit (L/C) for large orders, and Trade Assurance for verified buyers. For first-time buyers, we typically require 30-50% advance payment with the balance before shipping. Established clients may qualify for more flexible payment terms.

Is my payment secure? +

Absolutely. All transactions are conducted through secure banking channels. We provide official invoices, receipts, and can work with escrow services or trade insurance for your peace of mind. We're committed to transparent and secure business practices.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders? +

Yes! We offer competitive pricing for bulk purchases. The more you order, the better the rate. Long-term contracts and repeat customers also qualify for special pricing. Contact us with your volume requirements for a customized quote.

Can I visit your facilities? +

Yes, we welcome serious buyers to visit our facilities and farms in Nigeria. This allows you to see our operations, meet our team, and verify our quality standards firsthand. Please schedule visits in advance so we can arrange proper accommodation and farm tours.

What makes Snowdon23 different from other suppliers? +

Our competitive advantages include: Direct relationships with farmers (no middlemen), Rigorous quality control processes, Transparent communication and documentation, Competitive pricing with no hidden costs, Reliable delivery schedules, Responsive customer service, and Years of export experience. We treat every client as a long-term partner.
